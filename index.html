About me

Greetings!
I’m Michal 'The Bear' Szymczak

I’m a Videographer & Video Editor based in Los Angeles. I have 3 years of experience working with professional cameras, including Blackmagic, Sony, Panasonic, JVC, audio recording and lightning equipment. As a videographer I know the ins and outs of post production workflow: editing, color grading and sound design. I’ve had the pleasure to work on short films, short documentaries, corporate videos, interviews, music videos, social media content, live events, wedding videos, promo videos and behind the scenes.

I'm constantly looking for interesting projects, so reach out to me and we’ll create something great together!
